Pancakes

The best buttermilk pancakes ever!

Who doesn't love buttermilk pancakes? Sweet, fluffy, tangy, yummy - this breakfast treat is sure to please even the pickiest of eaters

Buttermilk pancakes can even be made vegan by substituting the buttermilk with almond milk and mixing in 1 tablespoon of white vinegar

Ingredients

  • 2 C All-Purpose Flour
  • 3 TBSP Sugar
  • 2 TSP Baking Powder
  • 1 TSP Kosher Salt
  • 2 C Buttermilk
  • 2 Eggs (or 100g canola oil if vegan)
  • Maple syrup for serving

Steps

  1. Whisk together all of the ingredients, being careful not to overmix

  2. Preheat griddle to 325deg. Spray griddle with nonstick spray. Pour 1 cup of pancake batter on griddle and wait for bubbles. Once bubbly, flip pancakes and wait for second side to cook.

  3. Continue this process until all of your batter is gone. Serve up your delicious pancakes and cover with maple syrup!
