Chocolate Cake

The best chocolate cake ever!

This chocolate cake recipe can't be beat. It is rich with a light crumb.

It is infitiely adaptable: add jam to the center, make the frosting whipped cream, or top with berries. This cake is your canvas!

Ingredients

  • 2 C White Sugar
  • 2 C All Purpose Flour
  • 1 C Unsweetened Cocoa Powder
  • 3 TSP Baking Powder
  • 1 TSP Salt
  • 1 C Milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 C Canola Oil
  • 2 TSP Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350deg. Spray 2 9in. orund baking pans with nonstick spray.
  2. In a large bowl stir together all dry ingredients. Add all wet ingredients and mix until just combined. Pour into prepared pans
  3. Bake 30 to 40 minutes, or until a toothpick ocmes out clean, remove from pans and move to wire rack to cool completely.
